Homemade Icecream

This is homemade icecream.

Ingredients

  • Heavy Cream
  • Sale
  • Ice
  • 2 Large Ziplocks
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Open your favorite wine and turn on some music
  2. Place salt and ice in a large ziplock bag
  3. Insert the second ziplock and add heavy cream, sugar, and vanilla
  4. Shake until frozen!
