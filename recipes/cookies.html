Chocolate Chip

This is a chocolate chip cookie. Mix oil, vanilla, milk, and 2 eggs. Add a stick of butter. Add one cup of flower and a half cup of sugar and teaspoon of salt. Mix. Bake for 9 minutes at 450F.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Open your favorite wine and turn on some music
  2. Preheat oven to 400F
  3. Mix oil, eggs, butter, vanilla extract in a bowl
  4. In a separate bowl, mix flour and salt
  5. Add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients while mixing
  6. Add chocolate chips
  7. Scoop to 1 inch balls on a baking sheet
  8. Place in over for 9 minutes
