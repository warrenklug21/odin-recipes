Homemade Brownies

This is homemade Brownies.

Ingredients

  • Flours/li>
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400F
  2. Open your favorite wine and turn on some music
  3. Mix salt, flour, sugar in one bowl.
  4. Mix eggs, butter, vanilla in another bowl
  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  6. Pour into 9" square baking pan. Place in over for 12 minutes.
